Radarr (movies)

Sonarr (TV)

Ratings (TheMovieDB) — required

A saved TMDb key is required to scan — scoring uses the audience (user) vote average, scaled to 0–100. Radarr/Sonarr's own ratings are used first, so TMDb is only queried for titles they can't rate (TMDb has no daily request limit). Titles with no rating, or a rating of 0, are marked needs review and never deleted.

Overseerr / Jellyseerr (optional)

Optional. When set, anything mediacleanuparr deletes is also cleared from Overseerr/Jellyseerr so it stops auto-requeuing it. The title stays fully re-requestable in Seerr afterwards — this does not blacklist it.

Deletion behaviour

Dry-run-only is OFF. Confirmed deletions will act on your library.
